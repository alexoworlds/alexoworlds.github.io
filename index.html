Alexandre Branco

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Astronomy at the University of Porto and in the Planetary Systems group at the Institute of Astrophysics and Space Sciences.

My main area of research focuses on understanding the chemical composition of exoplanet atmospheres by combining atmospheric modelling with observations. I am particularly interested in studying the chemistry of these distant worlds to better understand how they form, evolve and what they can reveal about the search for life beyond Earth.
